## Decisions Made
- **Dual Engine Architecture**: Users can choose between Verify550 (paid, external) and our native engine (free, built-in)
- **Custom Roles over Fixed Roles**: System roles provide the base, custom roles extend with granular permissions
- **Audit-first**: Every state-changing operation logs to `audit_log` table
